FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free?

Yes — completely free, no ads, no account. Download and use forever.

Will it slow down my PC?

No. The app uses less than 30 MB of RAM and almost no CPU. It's designed to be invisible in the background.

Windows says it's unsafe — is it a virus?

No. Windows shows this warning for any new app that hasn't been downloaded by thousands of people yet. Click More info → Run anyway. The app is open-source and contains no malware.

How do I remove it?

Right-click the pet → Settings → scroll to the bottom → Uninstall. Or use Windows Settings → Apps → Desktop Pet → Uninstall.

Can I add my own character?

Yes. Pack your sprite frames into a .zip (see the README for the folder structure) and import it via Settings → Characters → Add Character.

Does it work on macOS or Linux?

Not yet — currently Windows 10 and 11 only. Other platforms may come later.

How do I contact support?

Email us at Support@desktop-pet.online — we usually reply within 24 hours.
